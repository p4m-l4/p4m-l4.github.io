About Us

Synergy B.I Solutions provides Business Intelligence as a Service (BIaaS), offering end-to-end data solutions for businesses, NGOs, and governments. Our model simplifies data extraction, organization, and analysis—removing the complexity of maintaining infrastructure while empowering organizations to act on insights.

Our Structure

We are built on specialized teams: Data Extraction (source integration), Data Organization (cloud-based warehousing), Analytics (algorithms & ML insights), and Client Support (ongoing assistance and training).

Our Services

Data Extraction: Integration from internal and external sources.
Data Organization: Secure, scalable cloud-based structuring.
Data Insights: Advanced analytics and machine learning for decision-making.
Consultation: Guidance on KPIs, strategy, and maximizing data use.
Training Programs: Tailored sessions from data literacy to advanced analytics.
Client Support: Dedicated assistance throughout every engagement.
