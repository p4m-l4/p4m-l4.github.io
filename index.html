FAQ

How soon can we get value?

Starter and Standard clients usually see dashboards within 2–4 weeks, depending on data readiness. Premium engagements can run parallel tracks for faster delivery.

Do you work with our existing tools?

Yes. We integrate seamlessly with Excel, Sheets, Power BI, Tableau, Looker Studio, and evolve the architecture over time to fit your needs.

How do you ensure data security?

We follow least-privilege access, encrypted storage/transport, and client-owned cloud resources where required. Snowflake and major cloud platforms provide audited security controls.

Can you train our team?

Absolutely. From data literacy to advanced analytics, training is included in plans and available as an on-site add-on.
